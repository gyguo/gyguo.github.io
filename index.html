Biography

I am currently an Algorithm Expert at Alibaba DAMO Academy and a Postdoctoral Researcher at Zhejiang University. Prior to this, I received my Ph.D. under the supervision of Prof. Junwei Han (IEEE Fellow). My research interests lie in Artificial Intelligence, with a particular focus on computer vision and medical image analysis. Currently, my primary research centers on multimodal AI systems for cancer screening, diagnosis, prognosis, and treatment planning.
